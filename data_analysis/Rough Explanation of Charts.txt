hard_drops:
	This represents the number of times the spacebar is pressed each turn the player gets to play. A hard drop is usually a good indicator for seasoned Tetris players that are confident in their playstyles.
repositions:
	This represents the number of times a block is moved each turn the player gets to play. (this includes movements to the left, right and rotations)
score:
	This represents the progression of the players score each time they get to play.
score_growth:
	This represents how much a players score goes up (NET) each turn the player gets to play.
score_over_game_time:
	This represents the progression of the players score over the course of the game. (turns they play and turns they do not play).
time:
	This represents the amount of time a player takes to play each turn in ms.
